About Us


The PeñaRito website is an interactive map affiliated with Peñaranda Tourism.
Its main function is to help residents and tourists navigate the local area of Peñaranda. The map shows specific subdivisions, including Poblacion I, II, III, and IV, Santo Tomas, and Sinasajan, as well as key infrastructure such as the Peñaranda Bridge and major streets like Sanana, Labasan, and Padilla.

Additionally, the PeñaRito website aims to provide users with a more engaging and informative digital experience by offering clear visual guides and accessible navigation tools. By integrating important local landmarks, food establishments, and community areas, the platform ensures that both locals and visitors gain a better understanding of Peñaranda’s layout. This helps promote tourism, supports local businesses, and strengthens community awareness of the town’s geography and cultural identity.

By combining accurate mapping with curated local highlights, PeñaRito stands as a modern tool that elevates Peñaranda’s tourism experience and invites more people to discover what the town has to offer.
